Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a spicy Filipino dish made with pork, coconut milk, and chili peppers. It originates from the Bicol region in the Philippines and is known for its rich, creamy sauce and bold flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 500g Pork Belly, cut into cubes
  • 2 cups Coconut Milk
  • 1-2 cups Long Green Chilies (siling haba), sliced
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Shrimp Paste (bagoong)
  • 1 tablespoon Fish Sauce (patis)
  • 1 teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • Salt to taste
